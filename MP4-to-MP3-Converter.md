# MP4 to MP3 Converter
---
This is is a very simple **MP4 to MP3 Converter** made with the [Moviepy](https://pypi.org/project/moviepy/) module and [PyQt5](https://doc.qt.io/archives/qtforpython-5/) as the UI.

It uses [ffmpeg](https://www.ffmpeg.org/), a popular Video and Audio tool, to convert the Video file into an Audio file.
